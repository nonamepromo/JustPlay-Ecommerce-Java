Dropdowns

Dropdowns are toggleable, contextual overlays for displaying lists of links and more. They’re made interactive with the included Gameforest dropdown JavaScript plugin.

Example

Toggle contextual overlays for displaying lists of links and more with the Gameforest dropdown plugin. They’re toggled by clicking, not by hovering; this is an intentional design decision.

Dropdown styles

By default, a dropdown menu is automatically positioned 100% from the top and along the left side of its parent. Add .dropdown-menu-right to a .dropdown-menu to right align the dropdown menu.
